Welcome to MR CHURRO LA

Mr Churro would like to welcome you to our home at the Historic Mexican Market Plaza known as Olvera Street (Calle Olvera) since 1974. Our food is made fresh to order including the churros which are made from scratch. In 1999 Mr Churro introduced filled churros with three flavors Cajeta (caramel), Fresa (strawberry) and Vanilla (custard) and eventually added chocolate, guava, cheesecake, lechera and apple.Come join us and enjoy our traditional street desert found on almost every corner in Mexico!
